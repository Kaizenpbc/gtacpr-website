What Is ESL CPR Training?

The Same Certification. Taught in a Way That Works for You.

Our ESL CPR and First Aid classes follow the same WSIB Approved curriculum as our regular courses — you receive the exact same certificate. The difference is how we teach it.

Our instructors are fluent in your language. They explain medical terms in plain words, demonstrate techniques clearly, and give you the time and space to ask questions without feeling rushed.

No prior English knowledge is required to attend. Materials are available in multiple languages.

  • Taught by fluent bilingual instructors
  • Multilingual course materials available
  • Small class sizes for more one-on-one time
  • Same WSIB Approved certificate as all other classes
  • No English level requirement to attend
Who Is It For?

For Anyone Who Learns Better in Their Own Language

Our ESL classes are open to everyone — you don't need to be a newcomer or have limited English. Many of our students simply feel more confident learning medical skills in their first language.

We regularly train newcomers to Canada, healthcare workers, parents, community volunteers, and employees whose workplace requires certification — all in the language that helps them learn best.

  • Newcomers to Canada
  • Healthcare & childcare workers
  • Parents & caregivers
  • Community & religious organizations
  • Anyone who prefers learning in their first language
