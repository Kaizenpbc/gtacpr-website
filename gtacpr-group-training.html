Why Choose On-Site Training?

Bringing training to your workplace eliminates travel time, keeps your team comfortable in a familiar environment, and lets us tailor the session to your specific setting and industry hazards.

We Come to You

Our instructors travel to your site with all equipment — mannequins, AEDs, and materials included. No logistics for your team.

Flexible Scheduling

We work around your operations — mornings, evenings, weekends, or overnight. 24/7 availability for shift-based workplaces.

WSIB Compliant

All courses meet Ontario WSIB and Occupational Health & Safety Act requirements. Certificates issued same day.

Any Size Group

From 5 to 500+ employees. We scale the number of instructors to your group so everyone gets hands-on practice time.
